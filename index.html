How it works?

PHASE 1

Deep Self-Discovery

Answer anonymously 10 carefully designed questions and evaluate answers of others every day for 7 days. These aren't small talk. They're about your values, your past, what you want, how you love, what scares you, what lights you up.

Your investment: 20 minutes a day

What you get: Clarity about yourself.

PHASE 2

Online Voice Connection

Meet your 5 most compatible matches in a facilitated online evening. Cameras off, voices on. No pressure about how you look. Just real conversation, lighthearted questions, team games, and genuine interaction.

Your investment: One evening

What you get: Meeting people who align with you.

PHASE 3

Explore Real Life Chemistry

Meet your top 3 matches in person at a beautiful outdoor spot in your city. Structured adventurous walk-and-talks where you discover questions together, have fun opt-in dares and tackle the awkwardness together.

Your investment: One afternoon

What you get: Meaningful dates with real potential.

Frequently Asked Questions

When is this starting?

We are launching the first cohort in mids of January 2026! We will let you know as soon as possible.

What if I don't complete all 7 days?

That's okay. Life happens. Your answers are saved and you can pick up where you left off or try again with the next cohort. We open then every 1–2 months.

What if I complete the questions but don't like my matches?

You see anonymous reports of your matches are before joining the online and in-person sessions. If they don't interest you, you can skip this cohort and try again next time, or just take the self-knowledge you gained.

What kind of questions are we talking about??

Deep ones. About your values, past relationships, family, what you're looking for, how you handle conflict, what makes you tick. Some are lighthearted, some are vulnerable. All are designed to reveal who you really are.

How do you match people?

Based on your answers and preferences. Every step is designed to help you and us make a better decision. Some questions work best when people align (values, life goals). Others work best when people complement each other (personality traits, strengths). You also tell us which topics matter most to you.

What if I'm shy or introverted?

Perfect. We designed this specifically so introverts can shine. Not everything rewards being loud. We include activities that value listening, observation, thoughtfulness or your analytical thinking, all introvert superpowers!

What happens if I can't make the event dates?

Your answers are saved and you can join the next cohort's events. We run new cohorts every 1 – 2 months.

Is this only for people looking for serious relationships?

Yes. The 7-day commitment naturally filters for people who want something meaningful. If you're looking for casual dating, there are easier ways and BetterDates are not for you.

What if I don't find anyone?

Statistically unlikely if you answer honestly, but if it happens then you've invested time into understanding yourself better, trying something different and creating genuine connections. The entire process is designed so even if you don't find a partner it's still valuable for you.

Can I bring a friend?

Absolutely! You each go through the process independently, but it can be fun to do it alongside a friend.

What do I actually get from this?

  • Self-Knowledge: The questions force you to articulate what you want, what you've learned, and what matters to you. This clarity changes everything, even outside of dating.
  • Real Conversation Skills: Practice showing up authentically, listening deeply, and connecting beyond surface level. These skills serve you forever.
  • Genuine Connection: Even if romantic matches don't work out, you'll meet thoughtful, self-aware people.
  • Hope Restored: This experience reminds you that good people exist and meaningful connection is possible.
